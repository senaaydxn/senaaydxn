My name is Sena Aydın.
I am Frontend Developer and UI/UX Designer with a strong engineering background.
I bring creativity and precision to web and mobile design projects.
With experience in the e-commerce sector, I’ve honed my skills in creating user-friendly designs that drive conversion rates. By incorporating user feedback, I effectively revise designs to meet user needs. Proficient in tools like Adobe Creative Suite and Figma, I am always eager to learn and innovate. Known for being a collaborative and reliable team player, I excel at turning ideas into visually impactful designs.
In addition to my identity as a computer engineer, I have developed myself in the field of UI/UX design.
I was born on December 4, 1996, in Istanbul. I create designs in fashion design and share them on my Instagram page, sennasland. Writing, singing, and composing music are other hobbies of mine. I have received training in music production and sound design, and I am a music producer. My songs are available on all music platforms. I am also receiving equestrian training and I love animals.
I am proficient in using popular design tools like Adobe, Figma, Sketch, Webflow etc., actively and effectively. I believe I add value to the team I work with. I can turn every sentence into a design. I am dynamic and eager to learn. I am a collaborative and well-liked team player. I believe my visual skills set me apart. Since I also know the development stages of a design, this is a great advantage for the team I am part of and the tasks at hand.
